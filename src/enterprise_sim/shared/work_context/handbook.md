# Company Handbook — Office Furniture Co.

## Return Policy
- Items can be returned within 30 days of delivery for a full refund
- Items must be in original packaging and unused condition
- Opened items may be subject to a 15% restocking fee
- Mattresses and custom orders are final sale

## Damaged Item Policy
- If a customer receives a damaged item, they must contact support within 48 hours with photos
- Arrange a free replacement or full refund
- Do not ask the customer to discard packaging until the claim is resolved

## Exchange Policy
- Exchanges can be made within 30 days
- Replacement ships once we receive the original
- If the replacement costs more, charge the difference

## Shipping Policy
- Standard shipping is free on orders over $100
- Standard delivery: 5-7 business days
- Express shipping ($29.99): 2-3 business days
- Oversized items (desks, chairs) may require additional 2-3 days
- International: US and Canada only. Canada orders take 7-14 business days and may have customs duties

## Refund Processing
- Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days after receiving the returned item
- Credit card refunds may take an additional 3-5 business days to appear on statement

## Warranty Information
- All furniture products: 5-year limited warranty covering manufacturing defects
- Accessories: 1-year warranty
- Warranty does not cover normal wear and tear or damage from misuse

## Order Cancellation
- Orders can be cancelled for free within 2 hours of placement
- After 2 hours: $25 cancellation fee applies
- Orders that have shipped cannot be cancelled — initiate a return instead

## Discount Codes
- WELCOME10: 10% off first order
- ERGOSAVE20: 20% off ergonomic chairs
- BUNDLE15: 15% off orders with 3+ items
- SORRY15: 15% courtesy discount for service issues

## Payment Methods
- Visa, Mastercard, Amex, Discover, PayPal, Apple Pay
- Financing via Affirm for orders over $200 (0% APR for 6 months)

## VIP Customer Benefits
- Priority support (response within 30 minutes)
- Free express shipping on all orders
- Extended 60-day return window
- Exclusive early access to new products

## Contact Hours
- Monday-Friday: 8AM-8PM PT
- Saturday: 9AM-5PM PT
- Average response time: under 2 hours during business hours

## Bulk Orders
- 5+ units of same item: 10% discount
- 10+ units: 15% discount
- Contact sales for custom quotes on larger orders
